Why Savvy Dealer Outperforms the Competition

Real data. Real results. No excuses. Here's what makes Savvy Dealer different—backed by numbers, not marketing fluff.

By Savvy Dealer Team

The automotive digital marketing space is crowded. Every vendor promises better results, smarter targeting, and revolutionary technology. So how do you separate the real performers from the noise?

You look at the data.

At Savvy Dealer, we've spent 13 years building our reputation on one principle: outsmart, not outspend. While other companies chase bigger budgets, we focus on eliminating waste and proving ROI. Here's what makes us different—backed by numbers, not marketing fluff.

We Tested 100 Dealer Websites. The Results Were Alarming.

We recently ran a comprehensive analysis of 100 dealer websites from the most popular website providers in the industry. These aren't small players—these are established platforms serving thousands of dealerships across North America.

The results were shocking:

  • Less than 50% passed Google's Core Web Vitals assessment
  • Average performance score: 49 out of 100
  • Highest score found across all 100 sites: 68
  • Number of sites scoring 80 or above: Zero.

Here's what makes this even more frustrating: Your website should be a competitive advantage, not a liability. Yet many dealer platforms were never built for speed in the first place. They're still running on architecture from a different era while Google's standards keep rising.

The major dealer website providers are delivering subpar performance—and most dealers have no idea their site is failing Google's tests and costing them traffic.

Savvy Dealer's standard: Every website we build scores 80+ on performance and passes all Core Web Vitals. No exceptions.

Why does this matter? Google uses Core Web Vitals as a ranking factor. Slow sites get buried in search results. Shoppers bounce before your inventory even loads. Every visitor who leaves is a missed opportunity—and you paid to get them there.

We've Been Optimizing for AI Search Since Before It Was Mainstream

Over 40% of searches now happen through AI tools like ChatGPT, Google Gemini, and Perplexity. That number is growing every month. But here's what most dealers don't realize: traditional SEO alone won't get you found in these AI-powered search engines.

When a shopper asks ChatGPT "Which dealership near me has the best selection?" or "Where should I buy a used truck in Tampa?"—does your dealership show up in the answer?

For most dealers, the answer is no. Their websites literally block AI crawlers without even knowing it, making them completely invisible to generative search engines.

That's where Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) comes in.

The Savvy Dealer team has been hard at work on GEO for over a year. Most companies are just now scrambling to package this as their "latest product" and charge extra for it. We've already baked it into every client's strategy—because when our clients win, we win.

Traditional SEO gets you found in search results. GEO gets you quoted by AI assistants as the trusted answer. Together, they dominate both worlds.

Probably not.

If you've ever wanted your data to just work without demanding your constant attention, this is what you've been waiting for.
